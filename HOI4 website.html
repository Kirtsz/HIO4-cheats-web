The codes:

First you have to know how to see country tags

  • 1.Press “~” on your keyboard to open the command console. You can’t do this in Ironman Mode.
  • 2.Type in “debug” and press Enter.
  • 3.Close the command console by pressing “~” again.
  • 4.Right-click on the country you want to see the tag of to open the Diplomacy menu.
  • 5.Hover over their flag in the upper right corner, and you will see their Country Tag where “current tag:” is written.
